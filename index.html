About Tarakape

The name “Tarakape” is derived on the Filipino phrase, 'tara kape', which essentially means “let’s get coffee”.

Our Coffee

Our company's coffee is made of beans found in Sagada and Benguet in the Cordillera Highlands in the Philippines.
